# eQ - electronic Quittung
## Potential Partners & Early Adopters

**Project Name:** eQ (electronic Quittung)  
**Website:** eqstandard.org

**Document Status:** Research Draft  
**Date:** 2026-01-31  
**Purpose:** Identify potential early adopters, partners, and allies for the eQ initiative  
**Geographic Focus:** Switzerland-first, then expand to DACH and EU

---

## 1. Swiss Strategic Advantage

### Why Start in Switzerland?

| Factor | Advantage |
|--------|-----------|
| **Digital maturity** | High smartphone penetration, tech-savvy population |
| **Existing ecosystem** | Migros/Coop already have digital receipts (Cumulus, Supercard) |
| **Strong fintech scene** | 393+ fintech startups, ETH/HSG research support |
| **Privacy culture** | Consumers value data ownership, aligns with eQ principles |
| **Multilingual** | Forces internationalization from day one (DE/FR/IT/EN) |
| **Small but influential** | Prove concept in controlled market before EU expansion |
| **Research backing** | ETH/HSG study already validates the use cases |
